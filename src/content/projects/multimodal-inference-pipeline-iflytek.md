This page focuses on my work on multimodal inference pipelines and ML systems in a healthcare AI platform.

- Designed an end-to-end multimodal inference pipeline integrating YOLOv11 perception and LLM-based reasoning.
- Fine-tuned Qwen2.5-7B using LoRA and RL-based optimization.
- Improved medical report acceptability from 40% to 67%.
